TheraVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

TheraVault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TheraVault mobile application.

Information We Collect

TheraVault is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not require account creation. Data you enter (mood logs, journal entries, progress) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Advertising

TheraVault uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

In-App Purchases

TheraVault offers a premium subscription ($3.99/month) processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment information. Purchase data is handled by Apple or Google according to their respective privacy policies.

Community Features

TheraVault includes an optional community board. Posts made to the community board are visible to other users. Do not share personal identifying information in community posts.

Data Security

Your personal wellness data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data transmitted through the app.

Children's Privacy

TheraVault is intended for users 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
